Your Smart Inbox: AI Email Manager

The Challenge: Why Email Is Broken

  • **Time Sink:** Manually sorting, reading, and triaging an ever-growing inbox.
  • **Stressful Decisions:** Constantly having to judge urgency and emotional tone.
  • **Slow Response:** Drafting professional replies is repetitive and time-consuming.

The Solution: Your AI Email Assistant

  • **AI-Powered Insight:** Automated analysis of every message for instant context.
  • **Prioritized Action:** Clear scores and moods tell you *exactly* what to focus on.
  • **Instant Communication:** Generate a perfect, personalized reply in one click.

Core Feature Breakdown

🎯 Smart Priority Scoring (1-10)

Our AI instantly analyzes content, deadlines, and language to assign an **urgency score from 1 (Low) to 10 (Critical)**. This feature eliminates the guesswork and directs your attention to the emails that truly matter.

😊 Sender Mood Analysis

AI detects the sender's emotional tone—such as **Stressed, Happy, Angry, or Neutral**. This insight ensures your drafted AI response is perfectly calibrated to the situation.

💬 Professional AI Responses

Based on the email's content and your personal **Q&A settings**, the manager generates a professional reply draft. Simply review, copy, and send—reducing response time from minutes to seconds.
